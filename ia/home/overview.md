# Home

## Purpose

Act as the OptiFinish brand hub entry point.
The homepage should establish trust, explain the portfolio clearly, and route visitors into the right product or service area.

## Primary Jobs

- communicate what OptiFinish is
- show the full portfolio breadth without overwhelming the visitor
- highlight proprietary and manufactured strengths
- surface trust signals quickly
- direct enquiries into the correct CTA flow

## Key Content Blocks

- hero
- portfolio overview
- manufactured products highlight
- automation highlight
- facility trust section
- client and partner proof
- Our Work preview
- contact CTA

## Notes

The homepage should borrow the storytelling confidence of the Z-TAP site, but should remain a hub rather than a single-product long-scroll page.
